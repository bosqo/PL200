## How to Use These Resources Effectively

### Create a Study Schedule
1. Block out study time daily (2-3 hours)
2. Mix resource types (video, reading, practice)
3. Include hands-on time daily
4. Track progress with practice test scores
5. Adjust weak areas dynamically

### Track Your Progress
- Keep note of scores on practice tests
- Track topics where you score < 80%
- Identify patterns in wrong answers
- Focus deep-dive study on weak areas
- Retake practice tests after reviewing weak areas

### Active Learning Strategy
- Don't just watch videos - take notes
- Don't just read - summarize concepts
- Don't just read questions - explain answers
- Practice hands-on - build things multiple ways
- Teach others - explain concepts to someone else
